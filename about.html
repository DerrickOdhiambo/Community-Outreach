Our Story

CPSP is a Self Help Group(SHG) serving the needs of children with Cerebral Palsy (CP) and other disabilities.It was begun by a mother who had her first child with cerebral palsy. Her situation of needing support to raise her one and only son and getting none, had her look for mothers with similar children. This was when she noted that a good number of mothers could not afford the frequent therapies, needed nutritional foods and medicines for their children. With that she began sourcing for support from friends and colleagues to help meet these needs a support program kind of. She began doing Cerebral palsy awareness by organizing CP fun days, then free/affordable therapy sessions once a month with the help of volunteering physiotherapists after which they were provided with home supplies basically food and pampers. She has created a community for CP children and families. However, our long term goal is to build a Support Centre, this will help free up parents/caregivers, to provide care and monitor kids progress.

Vision

Our vision is to provide a sfe and pleasant space where at least 90% of children in Machakos with Celebral Palsy can access fair and comprehensive treatment.

Our Aim

We aim to not only reduce the stigma surrounding people with disabilities but more importantly to provide treatment and support equal to those received by the rest of the society.

Our Services

we offer;

  • Occupational Therapy.
    We aim at helping the person gain independence in daily activities and routins in the home, school and community.
    Even driving is possible in some cases after this therapy!
  • Physiotherapy
    It uses physical approaches to promote,maintain and restore physical, pychological and social well-being within all environments of the child.
    In addition to mobility improvements for the patient it also offers preventative benefits, reducing a likelihood of complications and disability.
    • Speech Therapy
    It has widespread benefits for cerebral palsy patients. Not only does the process improve communication interactions, but speech therapy can also be used to strengthen and improve facial and oral muscle control.
  • Pycho-social therapy
    It helps in dealing with social issues associated with Celebral Palsy i.e anxiety, depression,sadness,confidence..
    The benefits to this are,improved relationhip skills between all parties involved, it reduces Isolation and helps in finding your voice.
