Thoughtfullness,empathy and careful consideration saw the initiation of the Celebral palsy support programme(CPSP)
haeded by Mrs.Everlyne despite the covid 19 pandemic it did not stop her from extending support to the family of Jackline.
Jackline is a mother of two boys:Alfonse and Antony of ages seven and six respectively.
Their mother Jackline double plays as their care giver and breadwinner
Unfortunately , the current global pandemic situation has halted her sources of income.We come together to help women like Jackline through the hard times.

Preparation for the visit to her house and other women like her begins two weeks prior to the actual visit date on the 6th June 2020.Mobilization of financial support from a few friends of CPSP helped Mrs.Everlyne raise adequate funds to
facilitate the donation of substantial food supply to women like
Jackline and her two sons.This helps such mothers provide a regular healthy diet that is of necessity to any Celebral palsy child.

Jackline and other women like her express their gratitude for the given support.
She also shares her story how the experience was raising two children with the condition.Mothers like her find it hard to maintain a regular visits to the doctor leave alone providing a good healthy diet.
