Volenteer Work

Donations is not all about money, but time also. Being part of the program will be a major boost not only to us but also to the affected just by spending time with them.

How can YOU make a difference?

  • Be part of the events, going to visit the caregivers and the children at a their homes to encourage them and spend time with them.
  • Creating awareness and spreading information about CP.
